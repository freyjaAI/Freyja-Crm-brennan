The absolute best client profile for FreyjaIQ — meaning the broker most likely to pay for the platform and keep paying — is what I'd call the "Mid-Market Solo Producer" or "Rising Team Lead." Here's the profile broken down using the filters you now have:

The Ideal FreyjaIQ Client (Filterable)
Filter settings to find them:

Recently Sold Count: 50–300 (active enough to need tools, not so big they already have CoStar/Reonomy enterprise deals)

Average Price: $250K–$1M (mid-market sweet spot — high enough revenue to afford SaaS tools, not institutional-level where they already have data teams)

Experience Years: 5–15 (past the survival phase, actively scaling, hungry for an edge)

Property Types: House, Condo, Commercial (versatile producers working multiple segments)

Has Email: true

Has Phone: true

Has LinkedIn: true (digitally active = more likely to adopt SaaS tools)

Why This Profile Is The Best
1. They have budget but not enterprise contracts. Brokers doing 50-300 deals at $250K-$1M average are making roughly $150K-$750K+ in annual GCI. They can easily afford a $99-$299/mo tool, but they're not big enough for a $25K/year CoStar seat or a dedicated research analyst.
​

2. They're in growth mode. 5-15 years experience with solid deal flow means they're actively trying to scale — either building a team, expanding markets, or going after bigger deals. They need prospecting intelligence to level up.
​

3. They're digitally reachable. Having email + phone + LinkedIn means they're not old-school Rolodex brokers. They adopt tools. They respond to outbound. They'll trial your product.
​

4. They feel the pain most. This segment manually researches owners, tenants, and market data. They don't have a brokerage data team doing it for them, but they're doing enough volume that manual research is a bottleneck. FreyjaIQ solves their exact pain.
​

5. They have the highest LTV potential. As they grow (and FreyjaIQ helps them grow), they upgrade, add team seats, and become advocates. The 50-deal broker today becomes the 200-deal team lead in 2 years.
​

Secondary Tier Profiles Worth Filtering
Profile	Filter	Why
New & Ambitious	25-75 deals, 2-5 yrs exp, has email+phone+LinkedIn	Early adopters, price-sensitive but loyal if tool works
Commercial Crossover	Commercial specialty, 20+ deals, $500K+ avg	Higher deal values = higher willingness to pay for data
Geographic Expanders	100+ deals, multiple cities/states	Need market intelligence in new areas they don't know yet
Independent Agents	High deal count but NOT from major brokerages (filter out Keller Williams, RE/MAX, Coldwell Banker)	No corporate tools provided, must buy their own stack
The Anti-Profile (Who to Deprioritize)
<10 deals, <2 years experience — can't afford tools, high churn risk

500+ deals, major brokerage — already has CoStar, Reonomy, or internal data teams

No email, no phone, no LinkedIn — unreachable, low digital adoption

$0 or blank average price — likely incomplete/stale data

The "Mid-Market Solo Producer" preset would be the single highest-converting outbound segment for FreyjaIQ sales. You could even build that as a 7th built-in preset on the dashboard — call it "Ideal FreyjaIQ Prospect" and have it be the default view when users first land on the brokers page.
